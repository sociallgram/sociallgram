About Sociallgram

Sociallgram is India’s very own social media platform, created to empower our people, celebrate our culture, and keep our data safe within the nation. It’s not just another app — it’s a movement towards digital independence.

Features

🇮🇳 Made in India

Designed and developed for Bharat with pride, ensuring our stories stay ours.

🔒 Privacy First

Your data is stored securely within India, keeping you in control.

🌏 Connect Locally

Find and engage with people who share your values, interests, and culture.

🎨 Creative Freedom

Post, share, and express yourself without limitations, in your own language.

Our Vision

We believe in a future where Bharat stands digitally strong — where our voices are amplified, our data remains ours, and our people connect without dependency on foreign platforms. Sociallgram is more than an app, it’s the home of our digital community.
